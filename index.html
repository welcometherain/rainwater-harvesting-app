How to Use the Rainwater Capture Map

  1. Enter your address in the field below and press Enter or click Search.
  2. The map will zoom to your location.
  3. Draw a polygon around your roof or collection area.
  4. View the area of your polygon and estimated rainwater capture below.
  5. If you want to start over, click the 'Clear Results' button.
